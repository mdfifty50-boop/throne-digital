Our Guarantee

We want you to be satisfied with our products. If you are not happy with your purchase, we offer the following refund terms.

Monthly Subscriptions

You may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase or renewal charge. Refund requests after 7 days will not be approved for the current billing period, but you may cancel to prevent future charges.

Annual Subscriptions

You may request a full refund within 14 days of your annual subscription charge. After 14 days, we offer a pro-rated refund for the unused months of your subscription.

One-Time Purchases

One-time purchases may be refunded within 7 days if the product does not function as described.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@throneapps.com with your order details and reason. We process requests within 3 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Charges older than the stated refund window
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations
  • Partially used annual plans beyond the 14-day window (unless pro-rated)
